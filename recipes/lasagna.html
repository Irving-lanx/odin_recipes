Lasagna Recipe

You can easily lighten up lasagna and save a decent amount of dollars by using cottage cheese instead of ricotta—you still get that same taste and comfort, but it's not as overwhelmingly filling and can be much easier to digest for people with minor dairy sensitivitie the cottage cheese with a mixer beforehand so it'll have a consistency closer to ricotta.
